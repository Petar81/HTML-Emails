A SOCCER CLUB YOUR KIDS WILL LOVE!

WLSC was founded by professional youth soccer coaches with a single mission - to provide a superior and consistent youth soccer program for an affordable price. We welcome youth of all ages to join our organization and master soccer skills in a competitive yet fun and family oriented environment.

“Both of my sons (aged 5 & 8 years) went to West Loop Soccer Club for a week this summer. It was the hottest week of the summer and I was ready to pick them up early everyday. Not only did they make it but they had a thorough workout, learned good sportsmanship, and had a blast. The coaches were great (all of them)! Now, my older son plays on the team twice a week — and loves every minute of it. This isn’t one of those dance-in-a-circle and sing songs kind of camps. They work on fundamentals, love soccer, and always make it fun. I’m beyond impressed with the entire program and am lucky to have found it.” – Wayne H. ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐
